TrackBP — Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 04, 2025

Summary

We do not collect any personal data. TrackBP is a local‑only app. Your entries (medications, blood pressure, pulse, and notes) are stored on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third‑party server.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account information (no email, name, or login).
  • No analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking.
  • No location data.
  • No usage metrics sent to our servers (we have no servers).

Health Data (Apple Health / HealthKit)

If you choose to grant permission, TrackBP may read your blood pressure and pulse from Apple Health to simplify logging. Health data stays on your device and is used only inside the app to display and manage your records. We do not store Health data on our servers, and we do not share it with third parties.

Payments

TrackBP offers an optional Tip Jar via in‑app purchases. Payments are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Children’s Privacy

TrackBP is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at danpellegrini63@gmail.com. However, TrackBP does not collect personal data.

Data Security & Retention

Your data remains on your device under your control. You can delete the app at any time to remove locally stored data. For continuity between devices, use iCloud/iTunes backups managed by Apple.

International Users (GDPR/CCPA)

Because TrackBP does not collect or process personal data, rights such as access, correction, deletion, and portability are inherently satisfied on‑device. If you have any questions, contact us using the email below.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.
